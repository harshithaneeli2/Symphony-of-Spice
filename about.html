Neeli Harshitha

CSE Exchange Student

From: Vel Tech University, India

To: INTI University Nilai, Malaysia

Hello! Namaste! Selamat Datang!

I am currently a Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) semester exchange student here at the INTI International University in Nilai, Malaysia.

Originally from India, where I study at Vel Tech University, this exchange program is more than just academics to me—it is a cultural deep dive. During my period of study here, I have made it my mission to explore the length and breadth of Malaysia.

From the bustling streets of Kuala Lumpur to the historic corners of Malacca and Penang, I am exploring the rich tapestry of Malaysian culture. As a food lover, I quickly realized that the best way to understand the harmonious blending of Malay, Indian, Chinese, and Indonesian influences in this country is through its food. That's what inspired me to create the Symphony of Spice gastronomy blog.

Follow my journey as I digest the code in the classroom and the incredible cuisines on the streets!
